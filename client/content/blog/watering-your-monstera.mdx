Monstera deliciosa is forgiving, but watering is where most beginners go wrong.

## How often to water

Check the top two inches of soil before watering. If it's still damp, wait a few more days — Monstera would rather be underwatered than overwatered.

## Signs of overwatering

- Yellowing leaves
- Soggy, dark soil that never dries out
- A sour smell from the pot

Stick to well-draining soil and a pot with drainage holes, and your Monstera will thank you.
