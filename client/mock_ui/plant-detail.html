Care Instructions

Light

Bright, indirect light. Avoid direct afternoon sun which can scorch the leaves.

Water

Water when the top two inches of soil are dry — roughly once a week.

Soil

Well-draining potting mix with perlite for aeration.

Difficulty

Easy — forgiving and a great choice for beginners.
