Our Story

GreenNest started as a small backyard collection of cuttings and a lot of curiosity. What began as a hobby turned into a full nursery once neighbors kept asking where we got our plants from.

Today we grow the majority of our stock on-site, from easy-care succulents to statement indoor plants, and we still believe the best advice comes from people who've actually kept the plant alive themselves.
